Data Fields

FLOAT32 first_passband
 This the low frequency component for the passband range, i.e. the passband (frequency components that are unmodified) is between first_passband and second_passband.
 
FLOAT32 second_passband
 This is the high component for the passband range, i.e. the passband (frequency components that are unmodified) is between first_passband and second_passband.
 
UINT32 sampling_frequency
 The sampling rate is 1 / sampling_frequency, which is the inter- sample time (i.e. delay) between coefficients of the filter. This struct models the impulse response of the filter and therefore the duration (or delay) of the filter is 1 / sampling_frequency * number_of_taps.
 
USIZE number_of_taps
 The number of taps, or samples in the impulse response.
 
FLOAT64 * coefficients
 The values of the impulse response.
