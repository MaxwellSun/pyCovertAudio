fir_passband_filter_t::sampling_frequency
UINT32 sampling_frequency
The sampling rate is 1 / sampling_frequency, which is the inter- sample time (i.e. delay) between coefficients of the filter. This struct models the impulse response of the filter and therefore the duration (or delay) of the filter is 1 / sampling_frequency * number_of_taps.
